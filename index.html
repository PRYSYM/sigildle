SIGILDLE

source · a PRYSYM toy · inspired by open-identity-symbols

How to play

Forge today's sigil — a sequence of four symbols — in six tries.

Tap a slot in the working row, then pick a symbol. Hit Forge to submit. After each guess the slots are colored:

  • right symbol, right slot
  • right symbol, wrong slot
  • not in today's sigil

A new sigil appears every day. Switch tabs for Practice (endless rounds that grow) or Zen (free-compose a sigil and share it as a link or image).

Keyboard: 1–8 place a symbol · ←/→ move · ⌫ remove the last symbol · ↵ forge. Mute with the 🔊 button.
